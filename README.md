# WeddingPlanner
## WeddingPlanner- Android Application for the 2019 IEEEmadC
<p align="center"> 
<img src="https://i.postimg.cc/C1F8fyKQ/1.png" with="600" height="400" /></p>
Wedding Planner is an application for people who are engaged and looking for buy or rent stuff for marriage; Wedding Planner provides many categories for clients include: man's clothes, woman's clothes, and many others also you rent cars, hairdressing and many also it's for the seller to let them reach more clients.


## Masters Team
We consist of 2 team members that make this application happen: Amr Telbani and Khaliel Assar We came up with the idea and have been working hard on WeddingPlanner starting from scratch. We use Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Sketch for UI-UX designing and Android Studio for developing.
In addition, Firebase, FireAuth, Firestore was being used for back-end development.

## Description
“There is no better thing than a happy marriage”

There is so many people are engaged and want to buy wedding stuff like clothes or jewelry, also they are waiting their best night in their life and most of them be scared of that something terrible happened or forget to buy something or they don't rent hall or a car or they rent them but they are awful, that maybe change their wonderful night to a very bad memories that still all over their ages.

also, there is a seller doesn't know what their clients want. Also, they can't manage their products good.

Here Comes our Application Wedding Planner to make the client and the seller happy and make wedding night the best night.


<p align="center"> 
<img src="https://i.postimg.cc/zfJz5qv3/4.png" with="400" height="500" /></p>

As the Seller, the decision is all yours. you will register and choose your shop category. 
After you join our family you will have many advantages :
you will able to add your new products, you will also chat with your clients to know what they need and what they like and what they don't. and because we care about you as a seller we make that a feature let you choose if your product is for sale only or it can be rented as halls or cars etc.., your clients will know the days that the product is rented so they don't rent it also you will see the rented requests, after client chat with you and you have agreed you accept his rent request if anything wrong happened you can decline it.
also, we care about our application performance so if you have a better idea please send it to us by using Contact Us. 
we hope you enjoy our application.

<p align="center"> 
<img src="https://i.postimg.cc/6pzmFKv9/555.png" with="400" height="500" /></p>

As the Client, the application is basically made for your comfort. you will register and join our family 
you will have many advantages  that make your marriage better and more comfortable:
you will see many categories that include all that you need for your marriage from clothes to cars to many things, you will be able to see all stores for this Categorie from your home and you are drinking your coffee you won't go to physical stores, you will be able to chat with any store owner about any product and discuss what you want. also, there is things maybe you cant buy "maybe you can" as car or hall so we make an option to make it more comfort as we said before you can see the rented days for a product and rent what you want from your home things you can rent a day on them like halls, cars, hairdressing, etc.. after you request a rent day you should chat with seller to get an agreement will response for you by accept or decline, also we are humans and we forget a lot normally, imagine you are going to marry you will forget too much, to we make an option for you to let you add Notes about your marriage with date you want to do it.
also, we care about our application performance so if you have a better idea please send it to us by using Contact Us. 
we hope you enjoy our application.
